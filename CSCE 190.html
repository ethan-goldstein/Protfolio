CSCE 190 Journey

Phase 1

Problem Statement

So many students have specific questions based on their content, but not every professor can answer specific questions.
Phase 2

Sketches

These sketches provide detail-oriented personas, based off three different potential app users. Each sketch is a different perspective of a customer using the app
Phase 3

Affinity Diagram

People may have questions about an app because of its functionality, user interface, performance, or compatibility with their devices. It is important to provide clear, accessible support to address these concerns effectively.

Prototype
