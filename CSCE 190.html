Project Overview

This CSCE 190 project addresses the challenge students face when seeking help with specific academic problems. Our solution is a tutoring platform that matches students with tutors based on the exact problems they need help with, rather than just broad subject areas.

Through user research, prototyping, and iterative design, we've created a concept that streamlines the process of finding targeted academic assistance.
